Cyber Security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, theft, and damage. It involves implementing technologies, processes, and policies to secure networks, devices, and data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction. Cybersecurity is essential for businesses, organizations, and individuals to protect sensitive information, maintain data privacy, and ensure the integrity and availability of digital assets.
